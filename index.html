About us

There's more to every athlete's story

Off Court Insider was founded by Charlie Kunkel in April 2026, built on a simple belief: there's more to an athlete's story than stats and salaries. Charlie has always been driven by a passion for community, charity, and the ripple effect that genuine generosity can create.

A lifelong sports fan, he started this platform to shine a light on the moments that matter most — not the championships, but the lives changed along the way.

We believe that when athletes and organizations use their platform to give back, something extraordinary happens. A visit, a phone call, a foundation — all of these acts of kindness from high-profile individuals can be truly life-changing. Off Court Insider is here to make sure those stories are heard.

MMA

The bravest fighter at UFC 311 never threw a punch

April 2026 4 min read By Off Court Insider

Photo by Zac Pacleb/Zuffa LLC

Jiří Procházka has never seen the walls of the octagon as the boundary of his purpose. When the UFC light heavyweight champion learned of Ashly McGarity, a young girl courageously battling cervical cancer, he didn't just send his well wishes. He flew her out to the Intuit Dome in Inglewood, California to be by his side at UFC 311, because he wanted her to know she wasn't fighting alone.

For Procházka, it was personal. Before the fight, he shaved his head, not as a statement, but as an act of empathy. A quiet way of saying: I see you. I'm with you.

His commitment went further. Procházka donated $50,000 toward Ashly's chemotherapy treatment and surgery, hoping to ease a burden that no young person should have to carry. UFC CEO Dana White, moved by the gesture, matched the donation in full, bringing the total to $100,000 and amplifying a message Procházka has been determined to spread: that cancer patients deserve visibility, support, and hope.

As of late 2025, Ashly continues her recovery, pressing forward through ongoing chemotherapy with the same quiet bravery that stopped a champion in his tracks. Procházka has spoken openly about how deeply her story moved him, how a young girl facing something so devastating with such grace reminded him what real strength looks like. In trying to inspire her, she ended up inspiring him.

Written by Charlie Kunkel on Friday, April 10, 2026 at 3:16 CT
